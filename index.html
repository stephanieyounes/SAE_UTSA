About FSAE

The Formula SAE competitions challenge teams of university students to conceive, design, fabricate, develop, and compete with small, formula-style vehicles. The competitions give teams the chance to demonstrate and prove both their creativity and engineering skills in comparison to teams from other universities around the world.
Events are broken down in static and dynamic:
Static Dynamic
Engineering Design Endurance
Cost Analysis Autocross
Business Presentation Acceleration
Fuel Efficiency
Skid-Pad







Meet The Team

Roadrunner Racing is a relatively small team currently comprised of 14 members who are responsible for designing and manufacturing each of the six subsystems: Aerodynamics, Brakes and Ergonomics, Electronics, Chassis, Powertrain, and Suspension. Each of which will require members to practice good time management, research and development and engineering skills.
